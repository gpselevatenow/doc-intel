## SECTION 7 - NARRATIVE

On Tuesday September 22 2026 at approximately 07:46 hrs, this officer was dispatched to the intersection of Sutton Street and Park Street in North Andover, MA on report of a two-vehicle collision with reported injuries. Weather at the time was a moderate rainfall and the roadway surface was wet. Visibility was unimpaired.

Vehicle 1 (V1) - 2018 Subaru Outback operated by Marcus B. Whitfield - was traveling eastbound on Sutton Street and entered the intersection on the steady-green-arrow signal phase. Vehicle 2 (V2) - 2021 Ford Escape operated by Ellen T. Park - was traveling southbound on Park Street and, according to witness John Smith and the V2 driver's statement, attempted to make a left turn across V1's path on a circular green signal without yielding to oncoming traffic. V2 struck V1 at the right-front quarter panel and passenger door of V1.

V1 passenger Jane Doe (DOB 08/02/1986) reported neck pain consistent with low-grade whiplash injury. Ms. Doe was transported by North Andover EMS (Medic 6) to General Hospital for evaluation. Drivers were not transported and declined further medical attention at the scene. V1 was towed by AAA Andover Auto Towing due to airbag deployment; V2 was driven from the scene by its operator. Witness John Smith was on the southwest sidewalk and provided a written statement in the patrol vehicle.

Driver Park (V2) was issued a citation under MA CMV 720, Section 3 - Failure to Yield Right of Way. No citation issued to Driver Whitfield (V1). Photographs of the scene, both vehicles, and the signal phase as observed were taken and uploaded to the case file.
